About

Welcome to RUN||actually

Our main objective is to get people off the sofa and enjoying being outdoors. We just love to run and we hope you will too after experiencing life on the trails. Having experienced many types of events, from 5km runs through to Ultra Marathons and Charity runs, we're dedicated to understanding what's important to you - the runners.

We hope to be the inspiration to help you get out your front door and achieve something brilliant. We have a lot of great events to choose from - so there's a finish line for everyone to aim for - whether you're a complete beginner or looking for an extreme test of physical and mental toughness, then click on the events button 'Search and Enjoy!'
